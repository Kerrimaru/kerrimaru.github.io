Memoria

Memoria was my final project I developed for my coding bootcamp. I am guilty of always having a million tabs open at once, because I always feel that if I close them I will never remember to come back to that page again. So, enter Memoria! A bookmarking app which takes a screenshot of the page you are viewing to give you that visual memeory jog, and saves it as a bookmark to your Memoria homescreen.

Memoria allows users at the click of a button store bookmarks into their homepage, complete with a screenshot of the page they are browsing to help jog their memory, the title of the page, and optional user-added tags. This works via a chrome extension which can be installed in the browser, and makes the ajax request to populate the user's home page. I built the app and the extension myself from scratch, with a rails backend and vanilla javascript frontend, and hosting on Heroku.
