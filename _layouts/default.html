About.

Work.

  • Memoria

    Memoria is a solo project I have been working on. It is a bookmarking app which allows users at the click of a button store bookmarks into their homepage, complete with a screenshot of the page they are browsing, the title of the page, and optional user-added tags. This works via a chrome extension which can be installed in the browser, and makes the ajax request to populate the user's home page. Please sign up and take a peek!

  • Go Chef

    Go Chef is a student project I worked on in a group of three. The app connects private chefs and caterers with clients who are looking to hire. Chefs can specify their culinary specialties in their bio, and also the location they are willing to work. Customers can search for chefs by skillset, and can leave reviews for chefs based on their experience.
